Project on console

Visual Studio is a app which lets you do text-based coding. You can set it up by downloading it on your computer, and opening it up. After that, You must make a new folder, and open it up in Visual Studio. You do this by clicking "File." You then select folder. After that, you click "New File" by hovering over the folder name, and put the files needed.

There is also a console screen. It is used for seeing all the contents of something. You get to it by double clicking the screen and clicking "Inspect." There is also a screen where you can see any text or numbers there. For this project, go to that place by clicking console when you have gotton to the console screen.
